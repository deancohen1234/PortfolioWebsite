Dev Blog 20

New Pastures Temporarily, November 4th, 2019

Hello! So I first want to apologize for not writing a blog last week. The reasoning for that was Halloween and a separate game demo I did at a local bar. And because of that I also wanted to break the news that development for Rejuvenate is going to be slowed way down for a while. The game I demoed at our local bar was a real hit and I want to be focusing my efforts on bringing that game to completion as quick as possible. This doesn't mean Rejuvenate is over, rather I am just going to be taking a break for development for a little bit while our other game is being worked on. I plan to resume development on Rejuvenate around March or April 2020. I want to really thank everyone who has read along with me on this journey and I will keep you all posted on how our other game is going. Thank you all so much!

Dev Blog 19

Playtesting and First Levels, October 21, 2019

Hello! So this week I finished up the first level's blockout. I also was lucky enough to get a friend to test the level and give some feedback. Overall he was able to finish it and also defintiely found some cheeky ways around the level that I did not intend for. It was great! He also mentioned that I should change how the character activates their wind gusts. Currently it requires two buttons, but he recommended that I condense it down to just using the right stick. I definitely agree with him and I hope this week I can work to implement that. I have also been working a secondary project with him that you can view on my main portfolio site if you are interested. Other than polishing and reworking the movement that is all I have for this week. I wish you all the best and I will see you next week!

Rejuvenate

Rejuvenate is a 2D platformer where a tree spirit must traverse through a dormant tree to bring back life to it. The game has a heavy focus on flow and serenity and is currently in development.
