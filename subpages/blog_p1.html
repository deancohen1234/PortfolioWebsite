Dev Blog 14

Cameras and Logging, September 16, 2019

Hello! So this week I focused on fixing the camera code. Previously I thought the way to work my camera was only move it vertically when the player reached certain bound in the viewing space. However that made actual vertical movement very odd and difficult. So busted out some games with similar camera movement and analyzed them. What I found is that the camera is always following the player, both vertically and horizontally, just at different speeds. I implemented that system into my game along with some extra falling code and that helped a bunch! I also discovered in Unity you can color your Debug.Log functions, which was wonderful! For this week I'm planning on adding a couple more background objects into the scene. And maybe planning out the first sequence of platforming jumps. Thanks for reading and I will see you next week!
