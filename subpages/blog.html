Dev Blog 1

Basic Framework Created, June 17, 2019

As of now the player is able to move around, jump, double jump, wall slide, and wall jump. These are the core of how the game will be played and as of now feel pretty good. They will most likely need some reworking but as of right now they feel solid. Currently I have been using tutorials online to help in the creation of the assets. Including a Udemy course on 2D asset creation which is where the awesome platforms come in. First 2D asset wooooo! Next week I plan to have another asset in the game, but mainly I want to make my secondary mechanic of having the player be able to transform and roll in a little tortilla. Wish me luck!

Dev Blog 2

New System and Block-Out, June 24, 2019

Welcome back! I hope days have been joyful at times! As for progress I have started blocking out the beginning layout of the game. This involves having the player start at the bottom of the tree and learn the mechanics of jumping and climbing to reach the top. I also added an entirely new system of bezier curve based water veins the player can ride. These are based off xylem tubes inside trees. They are made entirely from code and are very fun to play with. Upon thinking about what I planned for last week I realized that the tortilla roll just wasn't going to work the way I wanted, so I pivotted to the xylem riding. I also am still working on my 2D asset creation course and will hopefully have another asset to put into the game. Thanks for reading and see you next week!

Rejuvinate

Rejuvinate is a 2D platformer where a tree spirit must traverse through a dead tree to bring back life to it. The game has a heavy focus on flow and serenity and is currently in development.
